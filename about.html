Matthew Deatherage is an investment professional with over 5 years of experience selling complex financial and technological products to hedge funds, institutions, and High Net Worth individuals. Faced with restrictive policies surrounding entrepreneurship and development in his role as an investment advisor, he chose to leave his finance job to learn the skills to become a builder and a seller. Since then, he has worked on blockchain projects, developed Google Chrome extensions, written e-books, and worked as a writer for different industries in which he is passionate. This is his story.
